TollTheBell Morsecode Encoder/Decoder

Instructions

Morse code is a method of transmitting text information as a series of on-off tones, lights, or clicks that can be directly understood by a skilled listener or observer without special equipment. (Wikipedia 2014)

Usage:

Enter the text or morsecode into the corresponding (upper) box.

The correct translation should appear in real time in the box below. If not, click the button "transform".

Spaces between the words in morescode are symbolized with " / ".

The letters in morsecode are seperated with normal spaces.
